Welcome to Federal Medical Centre (Electronic Medical Records)

OUR MISSION

By efficiently applying Electronic Medical Records in the Centre, the EMR Unit shall reduce patient waiting time, ensure data quality and security and block all financial leakages.

OUR VISION

The EMR Unit aims to provide quality and timely clinical services to patients while boosting revenue generation at Federal Medical Centre in Abeokuta.

TEAM

Dedicated team of healthcare professionals, passionate about optimizing patient care through efficient and secure electronic medical records (EMR). We possess diverse expertise in system administration, data management, ongoing user training, timely troubleshooting, answering inquiries, and technical support to maximize EMR utilization. Committed to seamless EMR integration, ensuring reliable access to accurate health information for clinicians and authorized users.

Dr. OLUWASANMI KEHINDE

Head of Unit

Introduction

The Hospital Management Committee approved the establishment of an Electronic Medical Records (EMR) Unit under the supervision of the Medical Director on the 12 th of July 2022, to strengthen the EMR implementation process and ensure its perfect adoption in the Centre. This unit is headed by Mr O.O Kehinde and supported by 18 other staff drawn from various departments including HIM, ICT, Accounts, Audit, Laboratory and Pharmacy among others.
